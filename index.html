PART 1: KEYWORD RESEARCH

🔍Finding the Right Keywords

Tools to Use:

  • Amazon itself (search bar, "Customers also bought", "Related searches")
  • Helium 10, Jungle Scout, Viral Launch, Sellics, AMZScout
  • Google Keyword Planner (for extra ideas)
  • Competitor listings (see top sellers' keywords)

📊Types of Keywords

Short-tail Keywords:

1–2 words, high search volume, high competition

Example: "Pendant Light"

Long-tail Keywords:

3+ words, lower competition, higher conversion

Example: "E27 Adjustable Pendant Light for Kitchen"

Backend/Hidden Keywords:

Keywords customers search but not visible in listing

✅Metrics to Check

  • Search volume: How many people are searching
  • Relevance: Must relate to your product
  • Competition: Too high → hard to rank
  • Conversion potential: Long-tail often converts better

PART 2: KEYWORD PLACEMENT (Where to Put Them)

📝1. Title

  • Include main keyword + secondary keywords naturally
  • Keep it readable, not keyword stuffed
  • Max character limit: varies by category (usually 200)
Example: E27 Pendant Light – Adjustable Hanging Ceiling Lamp for Kitchen, Dining, Bar (Yellow Brass)

🔹2. Bullet Points

  • 3–5 bullets
  • Include high-converting keywords
  • Focus on features & benefits
• "Adjustable E27 pendant lamp, perfect for kitchen and dining room"
• "Supports LED, Edison, and globe bulbs up to 60W"

📄3. Product Description / A+ Content

  • Include secondary keywords naturally
  • Can use storytelling, benefits, and lifestyle use
"This retro-style E27 pendant light is ideal for lofts, cafes, and cozy homes…"

🔒4. Backend Search Terms (Hidden)

Accessed in Seller Central → Keywords tab

✅ INCLUDE:

  • Alternative names
  • Misspellings
  • Related searches

❌ AVOID:

  • Repeating title words
  • Commas (Amazon ignores)
  • Brand/competitor names

🖼️5. Images & Alt Text

  • Some tools pick up alt text
  • Use main keywords in image file names
e27-pendant-light-yellow-brass.jpg

PART 3: KEYWORD RESEARCH – KEY TOOLS

🔧MAGNET - Keyword Research

Helium 10's primary keyword research tool. Generates thousands of keyword ideas based on seed keyword.

What Magnet Does:

  • Generates related keyword suggestions
  • Shows monthly search volume
  • Displays Magnet IQ Score (opportunity rating)
  • Shows competing products count
  • Identifies trending keywords

Magnet IQ Score:

0-30
Avoid
31-50
Medium
51-70
Good!
71-100
Excellent!

Formula: Search Volume + Low Competition

🎯CEREBRO - Competitor Keyword Mining

Reverse ASIN lookup tool. Shows ALL keywords your competitors rank for.

What Cerebro Does:

  • Reveals competitor keywords (even hidden backend terms)
  • Shows organic ranking position
  • Displays search volume and competition
  • Shows which keywords drive sales
  • Identifies keyword gaps
CPR (Cerebro Product Rank): How many sales needed in 8 days to rank on page 1
• CPR 0-50: Easy to rank (LOW competition)
• CPR 51-100: Medium difficulty
• CPR 100+: Hard to rank (HIGH competition)

🔴 CRITICAL: For new product launches, target keywords with CPR under 50!

🧹KEYWORD PROCESSOR - Clean & Organize

Also called Frankenstein. Cleans and organizes keyword lists.

What It Does:

  • Removes duplicate keywords
  • Removes unwanted words/characters
  • Sorts keywords alphabetically or by frequency
  • Converts phrases to single words or keeps phrases intact
  • Cleans data before sending to Scribbles

✍️SCRIBBLES - Listing Optimization

Helps write optimized Amazon listings by tracking keyword usage.

What Scribbles Does:

  • Shows which keywords are used in listing
  • Highlights unused keywords (still need to add)
  • Counts character limits for Title, Bullets, Description
  • Color-codes keywords by search volume/importance
  • Prevents keyword repetition waste

Color-Coding:

🔴 Highest volume (use in title!) 🟠 High volume (use in bullets) 🟢 Medium volume 🔵 Lower volume (backend/description)

🔍INDEX CHECKER - Verify Keywords

Verifies if Amazon has indexed your keywords (= your product shows up for that search).

Why Indexing Matters:

If your keyword is NOT indexed, your product WON'T show up for that search!

How to Fix Non-Indexed Keywords:

  • If keyword in backend → Move to Title or Bullets
  • If already in title/bullets → Check spelling, try variation
  • Add keyword to A+ Content
  • Wait 48 hours and re-check

🔴 CRITICAL: Check indexing WEEKLY!

📊XRAY - Product & Keyword Analysis

Quick market research and product analysis tool.

Xray Shows:

  • Est. Monthly Sales
  • Monthly Revenue
  • Avg Review ratings
  • Top organic keywords
  • Search volume and ranking position

🌲JUNGLE SCOUT

Mainly used for finding good products and analyzing demand + keywords.

Key Features:

  • Keyword Scout: Find monthly searches & competition level
  • Sales Estimator: See how much competitors sell per month
  • Product Tracker: Track competitors' sales and price changes

✅ Best for: New product research before launching on Amazon

📈AMAZON BRAND ANALYTICS

Free tool from Amazon (only for Brand Registered Sellers). Uses real customer data!

What You Can See:

  • Top Search Terms: What customers actually type in Amazon
  • Click Share & Conversion Share: How many clicked vs bought
  • Competitor Analysis: Which competitors get more clicks
Why It's Powerful: Uses real Amazon customer data, not estimates!

PART 4: KEYWORD PLACEMENT STRATEGIES

📱Title Optimization (Mobile vs PC)

🔴 CRITICAL: 80% of shoppers use mobile! First 80 characters = MOST IMPORTANT

Title Formula:

Brand + Product Type + Key Feature + Size/Color/Quantity

Character Breakdown:

Characters 1-80 (MOBILE VISIBLE):

  • Brand name
  • Main product keyword
  • Top feature/benefit
  • Key specification

Characters 81-200 (PC/DESKTOP):

  • Additional features
  • Use case
  • Size/color/specs
  • Secondary keywords

✅ DO:

  • Capitalize First Letter Of Each Word
  • Use numerals (1.2m)
  • Include brand at start
  • Front-load high volume keywords
  • Use natural, readable language

❌ DON'T:

  • Use ALL CAPS (except E27, LED)
  • Add "Best", "Sale", "#1", "Hot"
  • Use special characters: !@#$%^&*
  • Repeat keywords unnecessarily
  • Exceed 200 characters

🔹Bullet Points Strategy

Character Limits:

  • Each Bullet: 500 characters max
  • Recommended: 200-250 characters per bullet

Bullet Point Structure:

[CAPITALIZED FEATURE] - [Benefit to customer] - [Supporting detail with keywords]

5-Bullet Keyword Strategy:

  • Bullet 1: Primary Keywords + Main Benefit
  • Bullet 2: Secondary Keywords + Unique Feature
  • Bullet 3: Long-tail Keywords + Use Case
  • Bullet 4: Material/Quality Keywords + Specifications
  • Bullet 5: Remaining Keywords + Warranty/Guarantee
🔴 CRITICAL: EVERY bullet includes multiple relevant keywords NATURALLY without stuffing!

🔒Backend Search Terms (249 bytes)

MAXIMUM: 249 BYTES (UPDATED 2026)

Hidden keywords in Seller Central that customers DON'T see but Amazon indexes for search.

❌ NEVER Include:

  • Keywords already in Title or Bullets (wastes space!)
  • Punctuation: commas, hyphens, periods, semicolons
  • Brand names (yours or competitors)
  • ASINs
  • Temporary statements: "new", "on sale"
  • Both singular AND plural forms
  • Misspellings of common words

✅ DO Include:

  • Synonyms: "lamp" if you used "light"
  • Alternative product names
  • Misspellings customers actually search
  • Related use cases
  • Material variations
  • Abbreviations
  • Different language terms

How to Format:

✅ CORRECT: hanging lamp ceiling fixture adjustable vintage retro farmhouse
❌ WRONG: hanging lamp, ceiling fixture, adjustable, vintage
Format: keyword1 keyword2 keyword3 keyword4
Just use SPACES between keywords. NO commas, NO punctuation.

🔴 CRITICAL: For German market: "Hängelampe" = 11 bytes (not 10!) because ä = 2 bytes

📄Description Keywords

Character Limit:

  • Maximum: 2000 characters
  • Recommended: 1500-1800 characters (better readability)

Description Purpose:

  • Adding ALL remaining keywords that didn't fit in title/bullets
  • Telling your product story
  • Including technical specifications
  • Providing usage instructions
  • Building trust and credibility

Description Keyword Strategy:

  • Paragraph 1: Hook with Keywords (start with customer problem)
  • Paragraph 2-3: Features with Keywords
  • Paragraph 4: Specifications with Keywords

➕A+ Content Keywords

⚠️ Only for Brand Registry Sellers

Why A+ Content?

  • Boosts conversion by 5–10%
  • Keywords allowed in image text
  • Up to 7 keyword-rich modules
  • Indexed by Amazon search (2026)
  • Image ALT text now indexed by Rufus AI

A+ Keyword Placement:

  • Module 1: Brand Story - Use brand & quality keywords
  • Module 2–4: Product Features - Feature keywords in images & captions
  • Module 5–6: Use Cases - Keyword-rich scenes

🖼️Image Keywords & Alt Text

Image Requirements:

  • Main Image: Pure white background RGB(255,255,255)
  • Product fills 85% of frame
  • Minimum: 1000x1000 pixels (Recommended: 2000x2000)
  • Use all 9 image slots

How Keywords Work in Images (2026 Update):

  • Method 1: Image File Names (before upload)
  • Method 2: Image Alt Text (CRITICAL 2026!)
  • Method 3: Infographic Text Overlays
Why Image Keywords Matter in 2026:
• Rufus AI analyzes images visually AND reads alt text
• Google Image Search indexes alt text
• Accessibility for visually impaired customers
• Better understanding of product context

PART 5: 2026 NEW UPDATES

🤖Rufus AI Optimization

MOST IMPORTANT 2026 CHANGE!
Rufus is Amazon's generative AI shopping assistant. By 2026, over 250 million customers actively use Rufus to search and shop.

What Rufus Changes for Keywords:

  • Semantic Understanding > Exact Match keywords
  • Natural Language > Keyword stuffing
  • Question-based queries > Single keywords
  • Context & Intent > Keyword density
  • Conversational > Robotic listing copy

How to Optimize for Rufus AI:

Strategy 1: Use Natural Language

Write like a human talking to another human, not a robot stuffing keywords.

Strategy 2: Answer Questions in Listing

Include 8-12 common customer questions naturally in bullets/description

Strategy 3: Complete ALL Product Attributes

Rufus heavily relies on structured data. Fill EVERY field in Seller Central.

Strategy 4: Add Descriptive Alt Text to ALL Images

Rufus "sees" images through alt text. Be descriptive!

🔴 CRITICAL: Test your listing with Rufus! Ask it 10 questions about your product and see if it answers correctly.

⚙️Amazon A10+ Algorithm

Amazon's algorithm evolved from A9 → A10 → A10+ (current). Major ranking factor shifts:

Ranking Factor Old Weight 2026 Weight What It Means
Keyword Relevance 40% 25% Still important but not #1
Conversion Rate 25% 35% Most important factor now!
Sales Velocity 20% 20% Unchanged
Review Quality 10% 15% Recent reviews matter more
External Traffic 0% 5% NEW: Social/email traffic counts
Return Rate 5% 10% High returns = ranking penalty
CRITICAL: Conversion Rate > Keywords! A keyword with 15% CVR beats one with 5% CVR even if search volume is lower.

❓Question-based Keywords (Rising Trend)

With Rufus, voice search, and AI, customers now search with FULL QUESTIONS instead of just keywords.

Old Keyword Style New Question-Based Style Search Volume Trend
"pendant light" "what pendant light for kitchen island" ↑ 340%
"adjustable lamp" "how to adjust pendant light height" ↑ 280%
"E27 bulb" "what bulbs work with E27 pendant lights" ↑ 195%
"ceiling light installation" "how do I install a pendant ceiling light" ↑ 420%

How to Optimize for Question-Based Keywords:

  • Use tools like AlsoAsked.com or AnswerThePublic
  • Check "Customers also asked" on Amazon
  • Answer questions naturally in Description
  • Create FAQ section in A+ Content
  • Use conversational bullet points

🌐External Traffic Impact on Rankings

NEW 2026: Amazon now rewards products that bring traffic from OUTSIDE Amazon.

External Traffic Sources That Help Rankings:

  • Instagram/Facebook product posts → Amazon link
  • YouTube product reviews → Amazon affiliate link
  • Email campaigns to your customer list
  • Blog posts with Amazon links
  • TikTok product videos → Amazon link
  • Pinterest pins → Amazon storefront

How to Track External Traffic:

Use Amazon Attribution (free tool in Seller Central):

  • Create unique tracking links for each source
  • See which external channels drive conversions
  • Amazon rewards listings that convert external traffic
🔴 CRITICAL: External traffic with HIGH conversion rate = ranking boost!
